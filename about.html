About Frontline Healthcare Digital

Frontline Healthcare Digital was built from the inside out — not by a marketing agency that learned about healthcare, but by someone who lived and worked in it for over 30 years.

That means understanding what it actually takes to run a shift, reconcile a billing cycle, maintain a CAD system, and keep an agency's operations aligned across disconnected platforms. Most people who build healthcare websites have never touched an ePCR. Most people who understand ambulance billing have never run a call. We've done both — and everything in between.

Frontline Healthcare Digital exists because EMS agencies and healthcare organizations are being underserved digitally — not for lack of options, but because the people building their workflows and websites don't understand what actually happens on a shift, inside a billing department, or across a CAD system.

We do.
